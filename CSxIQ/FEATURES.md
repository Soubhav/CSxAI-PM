# CSxIQ — Agent Assist

Bring the power of AI to every care representative. Real-time guidance, instant context, and intelligent recommendations—so every member interaction drives better outcomes.

---

### Live Transcription
Your agents get a real-time, speaker-identified transcript that auto-scrolls as the conversation unfolds—no more scribbling notes or asking members to repeat themselves.

### Objection Detection
Instantly recognize when a member pushes back or hesitates. Real-time intent analysis classifies the objection so your agent knows exactly what to address—before the moment passes.

### Rebuttal Suggestions
Surface context-aware responses the moment an objection is detected—complete with confidence scores and one-click copy so agents respond naturally and fast.

### Live Call Intelligence
Unify transcription, objection detection, and rebuttal suggestions into a single real-time panel that keeps your care reps informed and confident throughout every call.

### Member Intelligence
Pull up a complete member snapshot the instant a call connects—account summary, product holdings, lifetime value, interaction history, and satisfaction trends. Your agents never ask a member to re-explain what happened last time.

### Intelligent Recommendations
Surface data-driven product suggestions with propensity scores and clear reasoning that update live as the call progresses—turning routine service calls into growth opportunities without feeling pushy.

### Recommendation Cards
Present every suggestion as a clear, prioritized card with talking points your agents can use verbatim—plus dismiss and pin controls to keep the view focused on what matters.

### Post-Call Intelligence
Automatically generate call summaries, action items, follow-up reminders, CRM notes, and sales insights the moment a call ends—eliminating after-call work entirely.
