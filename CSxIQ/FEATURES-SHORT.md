# CSxIQ — Feature Summary

CSxIQ is a real-time call intelligence platform that empowers agents with live AI assistance — from instant transcription and objection detection to personalized recommendations — turning every conversation into a seamless, high-impact customer experience.

1. **Live Call Intelligence** — Real-time transcription, objection detection, and rebuttal suggestions — all in one unified agent assist panel.
2. **Smart Recommendations** — AI-powered product suggestions with confidence scores that update dynamically as the conversation unfolds.
3. **Customer Intelligence** — Complete customer profiles, interaction history, and satisfaction trends surfaced instantly during every call.
4. **Post-Call Automation** — Auto-generated summaries, action items, and CRM notes so agents can move to the next call faster.
5. **Objection Handling** — Instant recognition of customer pushback with context-aware responses agents can deliver with one click.
6. **Integrations** — Connect seamlessly with your CRM, ticketing, and workforce management tools for a unified workflow.
