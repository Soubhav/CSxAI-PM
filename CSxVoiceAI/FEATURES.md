# CSxVoiceAI — Core Platform

Build, deploy, and continuously improve AI voice agents purpose-built for your organization. Always available, always accurate, always on brand.

---

### Dashboard
Your command center for every voice operation—live call volume, resolution rates, member sentiment, and AI-driven insights, all updated in real time so you know exactly where you stand before the branch opens.

### Insights
Measure what matters and know exactly what to fix. A clear waterfall view ranks every improvement opportunity by resolution rate impact—so your team focuses on the changes that move the needle most.

### Agent Canvas
Empower your CX team to shape agent behavior without writing a line of code. An intuitive policy tree lets you edit prompts, set guardrails, and manage rules—then deploy changes in minutes, not weeks.

### Credit Union Scenarios
Purpose-built conversation flows for the calls your members make most—balance inquiries, loan status checks, card disputes, payment due dates, and more. Each scenario is tuned to how credit unions actually operate.

### Escalation Workflow
Ensure no member falls through the cracks. Intelligent routing handles warm transfers, after-hours alerts, fraud priority queues, and live agent briefs—so escalations feel seamless, not like starting over.

### Call Logs
Inspect any completed call with full transcripts, signal scores, AI-generated summaries, and audio playback. Filter, search, and drill into the details that matter—all in one place.

### Live Monitor
See every active call as it happens—status, elapsed time, and real-time sentiment at a glance. Identify issues early and intervene before they escalate.

### Knowledge Base
Upload your rate sheets, product guides, and policy documents so your agent always answers with accurate, up-to-date information grounded in your own source of truth.

### Sandbox
Test any scenario in a live simulation before it ever takes a real call. Tweak prompts, adjust guardrails, and validate agent behavior with confidence—no risk to members.

### Evaluations
Run structured test cases against every policy update and track pass/fail results over time. Continuous quality assurance, built into the workflow.

### Tools
Configure post-call actions—SMS summaries, email recaps, payoff letters—that your agent triggers automatically. Members stay informed without adding staff workload.
